Hot Chocolate

Description

Hot chocolate milk and sugar with chocolate syrup, marshmellows, whipped cream, or Cool Whip to make even tastier

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Stir milk and sugar together in a microwave-safe glass or mug. Microwave until hot, about 2 min.
  2. Stir in chocolate syrup until completely incorporated.
